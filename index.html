    About me
    I enjoyed shooting film but quickly learned it was a hobby that I found to hate as a job. Lost with no direction, I ended up working different jobs, 7 exhausting days a week for a few depressing years.

    Fascinated on how difficult life can be and how much help people unknowingly need, I wanted to improve their quality of life. Unsure of how to achieve this, I found myself learning to code on my free time. My free time became not enough time and now I am producing applications make people's lives easier one line of code at a time.

    I'm currently free lancing, brushing up my skills in Android development, optimistic in securing an opportunity as a full stack developer position.
